ANIMACIÓN GROK IMAGINE

                    
MODELO: Grok Imagine (xAI)
Genera video + audio desde imagen estática.
Duración: 3-5 segundos.

FÓRMULA BASE:
Static camera. [ELEMENTO 1] + [VERBO]. [ELEMENTO 2] + [VERBO]. [ELEMENTO N] + [VERBO].

AUDIO: [GÉNERO], [ELEMENTOS], [TEXTURAS].

PRINCIPIOS:
1. Cámara primero (Static camera.)
2. Inventario visual (lo que YA existe)
3. Un verbo por elemento
4. Verbos presente simple
5. Audio separado
6. Sin adjetivos innecesarios

BANCO DE VERBOS:
Orgánico: twitches, pulses, breathes, writhes, oozes, drips, blinks, crawls
Mecánico: rattles, creaks, swings, spins, clicks, buzzes, sputters, grinds
Atmosférico: flickers, drifts, swirls, settles, fades, glows, rises, sways

AUDIO PALETTES:
Crust Punk: blast beats, distorted bass, guttural screams, vinyl crackle
Horror Ambiental: dark ambient drone, metallic scraping, reversed whispers
Circo Siniestro: broken calliope, children laughter, static hum
Industrial: machinery, hydraulic hiss, chain rattling, power hum
Lo-Fi: vinyl crackle, tape warble, muffled radio, room tone

ANTI-PATRONES:
❌ "mysteriously moves slowly" → ✅ "moves"
❌ "Camera zooms dramatically" → ✅ "Static camera."
❌ "Scary horror music" → ✅ "Dark ambient, scraping, whispers"

SUTILEZA > DRAMA
